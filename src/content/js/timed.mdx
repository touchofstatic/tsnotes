## setTimeout() will not pause execution of other functions in the functions stack

Because it's an asyncronous function.

## Why does setTimeout() call produce different results or inaccurate timing?

The timeout specified is a minimum time that the browser should wait before execution, not a guaranteed time. 
If the browser is busy doing other things when the timer goes off, the function will be delayed. 
Depending on precise millisecond timing with `setTimeout` is not reliable.

**You shouldn't rely on timeouts to guarantee the order of execution.**
